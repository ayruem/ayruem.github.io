On the Issues

- Environment

We live on Earth. A unique part of that is having an atmoshere. The atmosphere is composed of several gasses including nitrogen, oxygen, and most prominent to this discussion, carbon. Carbon traps heat that is re-radiated from the Earth after it bounces in from the sun. As the amount of carbon goes up the amount of heat trapped rises. Tempretaure rises.

Human life as we mostly know it thrives on fossil fuel use. The use of fossil fuels increases the quantity of carbon in the air and over time the temperature of the Earth.
