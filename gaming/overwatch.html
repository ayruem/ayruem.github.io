Reinhardt

THE tank

Reinhardt Wilhelm is your team's most important member. He wields his powerful Rocket Hammer and carries a strong Shield Barrier. At the frontlines you can clear the way forward and hold the line for your team. His Fire Strike allows extra damage to be sent off from a distance. His most unique ability is his Charge. A succesful charge allows you to pin your target and carry them away from their team. While pinned you can crush your target between a wall and yourself. Finally, your ultimate Earthshatter sends out a cone-shaped earthquake knocking down any targets in its path.
